Removing information that identifies you before sharing your health records

We take away information that identifies you such as your name as much as we can before your health records are used by organisations.

Using an independent review committee to approve who you can use your health records

Before organisations can use your health records, they must be approved by an independent group of people (an independent review committee) to check:

  • why they need your health records
  • who will look at your health records
  • how your health records will be kept secure

If organisations are approved to use your health records they will only get the information they need to know.

By using strong IT security

Your health records must be stored securely and protected by passwords so only the appropriate people will be able to see them.
