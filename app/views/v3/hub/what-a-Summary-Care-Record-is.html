Your Summary Care Record comes from your GP record. It tells health and care staff:

  • your allergies
  • your medicines
  • any reactions you've had to medicines

Health and care staff might look at your Summary Care Record:

  • in an emergency
  • at a walk-in centre
  • at a pharmacy

Speak to your GP surgery to ask to look at your Summary Care Record.

Some GP online services can show you your Summary Care Record.

Start using GP online services.
