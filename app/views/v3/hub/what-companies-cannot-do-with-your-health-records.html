Use your health records for marketing and insurance

Your health records cannot be used by the NHS or other companies without your permission for:

  • marketing
  • insurance

Share your health records with third parties

Companies have to sign legal contracts before they can use your health records.

The legal contract will say that information from your health records cannot be given to any third parties.
