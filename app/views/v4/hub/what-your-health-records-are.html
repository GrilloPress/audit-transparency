The different kinds of health records

Health services such as your GP surgery or dentist have their own health record about you.

The different kinds of health records include:

  • GP record
  • Hospital record
  • Vaccinations record
  • Summary Care Record
  • Dental record
  • Optician’s record
  • Sexual health record
  • Mental health record
{{ insetText('Information:', '

You may not have all of these health records. For example, you will only have a sexual health record if you have been to a sexual health clinic.

') }}

What’s in your health records

Your health records include your name, age and address.

Your health records also have information such as your:

  • health conditions
  • medicine
  • allergies and past reactions to medicine
  • test results
  • hospital admissions
  • lifestyle information for example whether you smoke

Where your health records are held

Your health records are held at the place you went to for treatment. For example, your GP record is held on your GP surgery’s computer system.

Your Summary Care Record is held on a national NHS database. Find out what a Summary Care Record is.
