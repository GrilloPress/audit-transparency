Use this service to find out who’s looked at your Summary Care Record.

Your Summary Care Record comes from your GP record. It tells health and care staff:

  • your allergies
  • your medicines
  • any reactions you've had to medicines

Before you start

To use this service you'll need:

  • to be 16 or older
  • an NHS login

If you do not have an NHS login you’ll be able to set one up.
