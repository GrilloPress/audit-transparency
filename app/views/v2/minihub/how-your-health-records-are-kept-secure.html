Anonymised means taking away information that identifies you such as your name and address.

By using an independent review committee

Before organisations can use your health records, they must be approved by an independent review committee to check:

  • why they need your health records
  • who will look at your health records
  • how your health records will be kept secure

If organisations are approved to use your health records they will only get the information they need to know.

By using strong IT security

Your health records must be stored securely and protected by passwords so only the appropriate people will be able to access it.
